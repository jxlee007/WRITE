# Novel Title Brainstorm: The Universal Saga

**Context Merged from Stories 05, 12, 13:**

- **Protagonist**: Thoulien Multiour, a four-eyed alien warrior from another planet. Wears white robes with golden edges.
- **Weapon**: A shape-shifting steel bar inspired by the Monkey King.
- **The Journey**: A 6-stage cosmic destiny. He awakens with no memory, experiences tragic love and betrayal in his teenage years, unleashes a vengeful rampage, and eventually masters his power to become a legendary warrior and king of the universe.
- **The Conflict (Stand Alone)**: He must stand alone against his four friends to save a galaxy and his lover's planet, resulting in a heartbreaking rift.

---

## 🌌 Category 1: Cosmic Scale & Destiny

_Focusing on the space opera aspect, the galaxy-spanning journey, and his eventual rise as King._

1. **The Universal Saga** _(Classic, working title)_
2. **Chronicles of the Four-Eyed King**
3. **Empire of the Steel Bar**
4. **Destiny in White and Gold**
5. **The Star-Born Sovereign**
6. **Awakening of the Cosmos**

## ⚔️ Category 2: The Weapon & The Warrior

_Focusing on the Monkey King homage, the shape-shifting steel bar, and his martial journey._ 7. **The Steel Bar King** 8. **Thoulien's Reach** 9. **Wielder of the Morphing Steel** 10. **The Monkey King of the Stars** 11. **White Robes, Iron Will** 12. **The Bar That Broke the Galaxy**

## 💔 Category 3: Isolation, Betrayal & The "Stand Alone" Arc

_Focusing on the heartbreak, the betrayal by his friends/authorities, and his solitary stance._ 13. **Saga: Stand Alone** 14. **The Warrior Who Stood Alone** 15. **Against the Four** 16. **Echoes of a Shattered Trust** 17. **One Against the Galaxy** 18. **The Solitary Crown**

## 🔥 Category 4: The Tragic Rampage & Emotion

_Focusing on the loss of his lover, the amnesia, and his uncontrollable wrath._ 19. **Blood on the White Robes** 20. **Tears of the Four Eyes** 21. **Forged in Frenzy** 22. **The King With No Memory** 23. **Rampage of the Broken Star** 24. **A Heart Shattered in Space**
