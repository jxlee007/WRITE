# Story concepts (cards)

There is realm known as realm of shads . Realm of shads hold creature
Known as sonapers. Sonapers are those beings who same characteristics of human personality
But they don't have a tangible form like humans. sonapers have the ability known as powers which is only unlocked when they hold the position of revis.There is competition held among all sonapers to become the next revis among others who can replace the former sonaper holding revis position .this competition is held every century and is occurred in world of humans. In this competition first the sonapers who want to participate are gathered.then they are pass through the tradition of making sonapers into card known as
Shoshinsha. These cards attained colours after being transformed from sonapers . These colours represent the 5 levels to become revis distinguished there power capabilities
Some are near to become and some had a long way to go. Then they are taken out from realm of shads and then the cards are spreaded through out the world or so called earth. The card automatically follows aura of the matching personality in a human.
