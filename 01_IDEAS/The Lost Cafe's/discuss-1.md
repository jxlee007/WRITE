### **Option 2: The Psychological Thriller Twist (Dark Ending)**

Arjun B sends the "Come home" text. Meera returns. Everything seems perfect. They embrace in their beautiful apartment. However, as the camera pans to the mirror, we don't see the successful Arjun B. We see the hollow, gym-freak version (Arjun A) staring back with a dark, satisfied smile. He hasn't fixed his life; he has successfully "stolen" the other Arjun's reality.

---

### **Option 3: The Sacrifice (The Ghost in the Machine)**

Arjun B realizes that as long as two versions of him exist, the glitch will continue to hurt Meera in both worlds. He chooses to "delete" himself from Reality B to stabilize it. He wakes up permanently in Reality A's lonely apartment. Across the street, he sees Reality B Arjun and Meera happy together through the window. He is the "price" paid for their happiness.
