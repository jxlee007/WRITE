# Vyapar

**Status:** Idea
**Format:** Film, Web Series
**Genre:** Crime / Thriller / Dark Comedy
**Working Premise:** A Squid Game-style story where a group of family and friends unknowingly become players in a deadly Monopoly-like power game — until betrayal and murder reveal who the real 'Maalik' is.
**Date Written:** October 21, 2025
**Inspiration:** Squid Game + Monopoly board structure

## Synopsis

A group of people who begin with normal social relationships — family, friends — are revealed to be unwitting participants in a structured, deadly competition modeled on Monopoly's property/power logic. As the game progresses, betrayals compound and a murder occurs. The identity of the "Maalik" (master/owner) who designed the game is the central mystery.

## Themes

- [Betrayal and Loyalty](../themes/Betrayal and Loyalty.md) — the player group fractures
