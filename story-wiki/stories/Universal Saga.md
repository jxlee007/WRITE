## Synopsis

A four-eyed alien warrior, **Thoulien Multiour**, is The Chosen One of his universe. He awakens with no memory and must journey through six distinct life stages, each escalating in scale and existential cost. Along the way he loses love, loses sanity, and is ultimately shaped by those losses into the being destiny requires him to be.

The story spans cosmological scales — from personal love to warrior arcs to a universe-level saga.

## Arcs

The story is structured as a **6-stage linear arc**:

1. **Love Arc** — Thoulien finds connection; this is what he will eventually lose
2. **Warrior Arc** — becoming who destiny requires him to be
3. **Saga Arc** (stages 4–6) — universe-scale conflict and reckoning
