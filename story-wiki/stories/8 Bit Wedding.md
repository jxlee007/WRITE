## Synopsis

A gaming satire short film that re-imagines a traditional Indian wedding through the lens of a video game.

Normal wedding moments are visualised with gaming commentary and 8-bit effects layered on top:
- Mother-in-law meeting the bride → announcer says *'Camera Trap'*
- Bride touching the feet of the mother-in-law → announcer comments: *'Final Move — Touching Feet. This move has long been forgotten, use it cautiously'*
- A senior lady arriving → announcer declares *'Ladies Man'*; touching her feet earns the *'Impressionist Skill'* achievement

The screenplay alternates between a normal wedding scene and the gamer's perspective overlay — 8-bit sound effects, achievement pops, and a game announcer narrating every social interaction as a match event.

**Tone:** Light, satirical, affectionate comedy rooted in Indian family culture.
