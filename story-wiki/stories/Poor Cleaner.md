## Synopsis

A war-trained soldier — an eagle medal holder (the highest honour) and sniper all-rounder — who is forced to live in dire circumstances after the war ends. Despite his past glory, he survives with hard work and dignity, working as a bus cleaner, living off his former calibre.

He is inspired by the spirit of Nawazuddin Siddiqui: understated, resilient, and formidable.

His story is rediscovered when he is found and chosen by 007. His military importance becomes evident when he single-handedly sweeps 50 outer-force operatives in a cold, high-stakes infiltration — providing sniper support, scouting, and cover fire.

Core Arc: From forgotten war hero to indispensable operative — proving that true calibre never diminishes.
