# The Lost Café

**Status:** Idea
**Format:** Film
**Genre:** Psychological Thriller / Drama
**Working Premise:** A man stuck in grief for five years begins dreaming of the happier alternate life he could have had — but the connection between realities is a two-way street with dangerous consequences.
**Date Written:** October 13, 2025
**Inspiration:** Song "Darmiya"
**Core Theme:** Moving on

## Synopsis

A man has spent five years paralyzed by grief — an unspecified loss. He begins experiencing vivid alternate-reality visions of the life he could have had. But the connection is bidirectional: the alternate life can reach him too. Consequence escalate from psychological to physical to narrative.
