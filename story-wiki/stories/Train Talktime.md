# Train Talktime

**Status:** Idea
**Format:** Web Series
**Genre:** Slice of Life / Comedy / Drama
**Working Premise:** An anthology series of 10–30 minute episodes where strangers share stories, secrets, and laughter aboard Indian trains.
**Date Written:** December 3, 2025
