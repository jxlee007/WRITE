# Master Shin

**Appears In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../../stories/Lvl-3-Power.md)
**Role:** Supporting — Trainer / Outside Card
**Card Status:** Outside Card Structure — retired legend

---

## Core Traits

- Silent, aggressive, brutal
- Philosophy of "raw hard power truth" — no tricks, no strategy, just force
- Trained [Rollnado](Rollnado.md), who later humiliated and defeated him to break his ego
- Exists as an Outside Card: participated in past competition but no longer active

---

## Power Levels

- **Lvl 1:** Earth Mover — earth-bending style attacks; manipulates terrain and rock masses
- **Lvl 2:** Rare Earth Mover — advanced/amplified earth manipulation
- **Lvl 3:** Domain — **Barren Lands** (Realm Expansion); an expanse of desolate earth where Shin's terrain control is absolute

---

## The Shin–Rollnado Incident

[Rollnado](Rollnado.md) trained under Master Shin and eventually turned on his teacher — humiliating and defeating him in combat. This act broke Shin's ego and is a defining moment in both characters' histories. Rollnado's gravity manipulation ultimately overpowers Shin's earth-based system.

---

## Relationships

- **[Rollnado](Rollnado.md)** — former student; defeated Shin to break free of his influence
