## Identity

Speed and Skatze are the **same character** — two names for one fighter who trained under [Grand-Emo Storm](Grand-Emo Storm.md) alongside [Sato](Sato.md) and [[Maskarray]]. The dual naming may reflect a street alias vs. a formal card-rank name.

---

## Role in Lvl 3 Power

Speed/Skatze is mentioned in [Sato](Sato.md)'s character file as one of Grand-Emo's disciples. As a fellow student under the same master, he exists in Sato's orbit — a peer, potential rival, or ally depending on arc direction.
