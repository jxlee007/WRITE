# Thoulien Multiour

**Appears In:** [Universal Saga](../stories/Universal-Saga.md), [Saga Stand Alone](../stories/Saga-Stand-Alone.md)
**Role:** Protagonist (both stories)
**Species:** Alien (four-eyed)
**Type:** The Chosen One

---

## Core Traits

- Four eyes — likely symbolic of expanded perception or cosmic sight
- Awakens with no memory at story start — identity must be reconstructed through journey
- Warrior archetype: physically powerful, but defined by the emotional and spiritual costs of his destiny
- The six-stage arc suggests he transforms fundamentally across the story — not one stable character but a continuously evolving one

---

## Arc

**Universal Saga (primary):**
1. Awakens with no memory
2. Love Arc — finds connection; this will be lost
3. Warrior Arc — becomes who destiny requires
4. Saga Arc — universe-level stakes; loses sanity

**Saga: Stand Alone (companion piece):**
- An alien warrior who stands alone against four friends to save a galaxy
- His choice to go alone splits him from his lover whose planet he is trying to save
- Suggests a moment of inflection within or adjacent to the main saga
