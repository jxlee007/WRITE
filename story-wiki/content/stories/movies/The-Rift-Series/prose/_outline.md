## Arc Notes

**Episode 1 is the discovery arc.** He begins knowing nothing — not that the skill exists, not what rifts mean, not that other worlds are real. Each scene gives him one new piece of reality. The last scene gives him all of it at once, through her.

**The first rift (Scene 001) vs. the rift that opens into his world (Scene 003):** These are different events. Scene 001 is him reaching outward — accidentally, without knowing. Scene 003 is the rift coming to him — the result of that unconscious resonance finally completing itself from the other side. She felt him before he understood what he was doing.

**She is not passive.** The rift opens into his world in Scene 003 because she opened it. She felt the resonance from Scene 001 and came to look. She orbits his planet in Scene 005 because she was already curious. She is not a discovery that happens to him — she is a person who made choices. Episode 1 ends with him in her world, finally understanding. She has been ahead of the story the whole time.

**Silent film throughline:** Every scene is told in visual space. No words, no labels. What the characters know is shown through what they do — how close they get, where they stop, how long they wait, where they look.
