# Scene 003 — The Reach

**Format:** Silent. Visual only.
**Location:** Space. His planet below.
**Follows:** Scene 002 — he has spent days mapping the feeling. He knows its direction. He knows it is real. He has decided.
**Purpose:** The first deliberate act. He reaches. The effort is visible, sustained, costly. Something in the dark shifts. The scene ends on the first sign of a response — not the rift yet. Just: something heard him.

---

He rises differently this time.

Before, the rising was exploratory — he was following something, unsure where it led. This time he rises with direction. He knows where he is going before he arrives. His planet does not diminish the way it did the first time, the slow organizing of its shape into a whole. He barely watches it. He is already oriented toward the space ahead.

He rises until the feeling is directly in front of him, close and warm the way it has been for the past three storms. He stops.

He does not close his eyes this time.

He looks at the dark ahead — at the nothing, at the occupied nothing, at the specific quality of a space that contains something he cannot yet see — and then he reaches.

---

The reach is not a movement of the body.

It is a movement of the world.

Below him, the planet shifts. Slowly, incrementally — the way tectonic things shift, without drama, the weight of it enormous. The eastern rim — the edge he has been standing at, the point of the planet closest to the feeling — extends. Not growing, not changing shape. Leaning. The way a person leans in when they are trying to hear something more clearly.

The atmosphere follows.

His planet's air — the specific weather he has built and breathed and cycled through for as long as he has existed — stretches at the eastern edge. A thin reaching of it, past the rim, into the space beyond the planet's usual boundary. Not much. Enough. A gesture, extended as far as it can go.

He pushes it further.

This costs something. He can feel it — not pain, but the specific exertion of effort when effort is honest and not certain of its return. The atmosphere stretches another increment. Another. The planet's lean deepens. He is putting everything in one direction and leaving the rest of his world temporarily unbalanced, and he does it anyway because the thing in the dark ahead is worth the temporary imbalance.

He holds the reach.

---

The dark does not answer immediately.

He holds it.

The atmosphere of his planet quivers at the extended edge — not withdrawing, not quite steady, sustaining itself on effort rather than weight. He does not pull back. He has decided, and the decision is a physical thing now, visible in the shape of his world below him.

He holds it.

And then —

The dark ahead, at the point where his reach is at its most extended: warms.

Not light. Not sound. A change in temperature so small it should not be perceptible, and is. The warmth he has been following for three storms, the warmth that has been ambient and directionless, suddenly localises — focuses — arrives at the exact point where his atmosphere has reached.

Something on the other side felt the reach.

Something turned toward it.

Not a rift. Not yet. Just: an answer. The specific warmth of a thing that was uncertain and has now decided to look back.

His planet steadies.

The lean holds.

The eastern rim, stretched to its furthest point, is trembling at its edge like a held note — asking a question that the dark has not yet answered in full, but has acknowledged.

He does not move.

He waits.

---

**Consequence:** The reach has landed. Something in the space ahead registered the effort and turned toward it — not committing, not advancing, but no longer simply being a warmth in a direction. It is now a warmth with attention. An orientation. Two things in the dark, facing each other across a distance that is very close to being bridgeable. The next scene is what happens when they bridge it.
