# Shoshinsha Card Realm (World Mechanics)

**Story:** [Lvl 3 Power](../../index.md)
**Type:** Idea / World Mechanic

---

## The Realm of Shads

A dimension entirely separate from the human world (Earth). It is home to beings called **Sonapers** — entities that share the characteristics of human personalities but have no tangible physical form.

## Sonapers

Non-physical beings native to the Realm of Shads. Their personalities perfectly mirror human archetypes (e.g., combat hunger, domain mastery, defense, manipulation). However, they cannot interact with the material world on their own. A Sonaper's powers remain dormant unless they hold the title of **Revis**.

## The Title of Revis

The highest, most coveted position among Sonapers. The Sonaper who attains the title of Revis has their powers fully unlocked. Only one (or a select group) can hold this title at a time. The Revis can be challenged and replaced, leading to...

## The Shoshinsha Competition

Held every century, this is the ultimate battle for the title of Revis.

1. **The Gathering:** Sonapers who wish to challenge for Revis are gathered in the Realm of Shads.
2. **The Ritual of SHOSHINSHA:** They undergo a transformation that converts each Sonaper into a physical card.
3. **Power Levels (Levels 1-5):** Upon transformation, each card attains a colour representing one of the 5 power levels, indicating the Sonaper's proximity to becoming Revis:
   `Lvl 1  →  Lvl 2  →  Lvl 3  →  Lvl 4  →  Lvl 5 (Revis)`
4. **Descent to Earth:** The physical cards (Shoshinsha) are taken out of the Realm of Shads and scattered across Earth.
5. **The Bonding:** Each card is drawn automatically toward a human whose aura matches the Sonaper's personality. The card bonds to that human — granting the human the Sonaper's power set.
6. **The Battle:** The competition then unfolds on Earth among the bonded humans, fighting on behalf of their respective Sonapers.

## Card Suits & Personality Mapping

When a Sonaper transforms into a card, their suit is determined by their core personality archetype:

- **♣ Clubs / Leaves** → Raw combat hunger, brute power drive
- **♠ Spades** → Domain mastery, discipline, realm-seeking ambition
- **♥ Hearts** → Balance of offence and defence, protective instinct
- **♦ Diamonds** → Intelligence, manipulation, betrayal, self-interest
