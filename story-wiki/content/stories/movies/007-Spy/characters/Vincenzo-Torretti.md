# Vincenzo Torretti

**Role:** Crime Boss • Indian Underworld Kingpin (Mumbai Syndicate)
**Status:** ACTIVE (Rogue)
**Operations:** Global Smuggling, Shell Companies, Shadow Communications
**Countries:** India (Mumbai), USA, Global Syndicate Locations

## Core Profile
* **Role:** High-level international crime boss with deep political connections in India. He is the elder brother of the gangster ruling Mumbai. Torretti allies with Subject Alpha in an American prison, forming a powerful intelligence and logistics partnership.
* **Underworld Power:** He has immense reach. Even in a high-security US facility, he maintains direct lines to his syndicate via a modified old-model Nokia phone, demonstrating that classified intelligence is always just a call away.

## Biography
* **Betrayal & Extradition:** Betrayed by corrupt Indian politicians who sold his operations out to the CIA and Interpol. Torretti was extradited to America and placed in a high-security facility.
* **Prison Dominion:** Established himself as the shadow leader of the prison block, running the inner cell system. He organized weekend riots as a "strength test" to evaluate new prisoners.
* **The Alliance:** Noticed Subject Alpha's anomalous behavior and combat efficiency during a prison riot. Recognizing Alpha's elite capabilities, Torretti proposed a bargain: freedom in exchange for Alpha's assistance in neutralizing the politicians who betrayed him.
* **The Bail Escape:** Following Alpha's coordinate arrangements with Marshal Bradley, Torretti and Alpha were released through a staged legal "bail" order totaling ₹80 lakhs. Upon landing in India, Torretti facilitated Alpha's escape flight to Russia/China, bypassing RAW surveillance.

## Relationships
* **Subject Alpha (Protagonist):** A tactical alliance of convenience that turns into deep mutual respect. Torretti provides Alpha with underworld networks, safe havens, and secure satellite communications.
* **Colonel Rajesh Malhotra (Indian Officer):** An adversarial relationship. Malhotra was connected to the corrupt political faction that betrayed Torretti, making them bitter rivals.
* **Mumbai Gangster Brother:** Torretti's younger brother who executes syndicate operations in India and managed the funding of their custom satellite network.

## Specialties & Strengths
* **Global Criminal Syndicate:** Access to international smuggling routes, safe houses, and falsified manifests.
* **Satellite Infrastructure:** Use of private communication networks launched by his brother to maintain un-trackable contacts.
* **Command Presence:** Natural authority, physical strength, and strategic intelligence.

## Timeline
- **2017**: US Extradition - Arrested and transferred to high-security American custody.
- **2018**: Riot Standoff - Tested Alpha's combat skills during a block riot, forming a mutual pact.
- **2018**: ₹80 Lakhs Bail - Released under a staged bail document and returned to India.
- **2018**: Extraction flight - Provided Alpha with escape flight trails to bypass RAW airport surveillance.
