# Dr. Sarah Kumar

**Role:** Behavioral Scientist • Government Tracker
**Status:** PRESUMED DEAD (Undercover Shadow Flight)
**Operations:** 25+ tracking cases
**Countries:** India, UK, Global Assignments

## Core Profile
* **Role:** Subject Alpha's college romantic interest and core emotional anchor; later becomes a highly skilled government tracker and behavioral analyst.
* **Vulnerabilities:** Her strict reliance on empirical records (which initially convinced her of Alpha's death) can blind her to alternate shadow operations. She faces a severe conflict between professional objectivity and lingering personal memories, leaving her open to strategic manipulation by her superiors.

## Biography
* **College Era:** Peak peer relationship and romance with Subject Alpha before his sudden enlistment and subsequent "erasure" from official records.
* **Professional Rise:** Believing Alpha died in the war, she channelled her grief into academia, specializing in behavioral sciences and pattern profiling. She was recruited by Indian Intelligence for global tracking assignments due to her exceptional ability to identify targets based on psychological markers.
* **The Hunt:** Assigned to trace an anonymous rogue operator in London, she gradually begins connecting behavioral patterns that match her "dead" college friend.
* **The Reunion & Flight:** Cornered during a building breakout, she is confronted by Alpha. After a high-stakes standoff, they escape together before the building detonates, faking their deaths to disappear from the intelligence grid.

## Relationships
* **Subject Alpha (Protagonist):** A deep, unresolved college bond. Her search for the rogue operative becomes an emotional and intellectual standoff between her duty and her hope.
* **Colonel Rajesh Malhotra (Indian Officer):** Her superior officer and handler. He exploits her college connection to Alpha, manipulating her into leading the hunt without disclosing the target's true identity.
* **College Male Friend:** Part of her original university circle whose war sacrifice deeply affected her.

## Specialties & Strengths
* **Behavioral Profiling:** Reading target histories and predicting physical movements through psychological analysis.
* **Pattern Recognition:** Identifying anomalies in redacted databases and international travel manifests.
* **Ethical Resolve & Resilience:** Operating with high integrity under immense operational pressure.

## Timeline
- **2009**: College Era - Developed a close relationship with Subject Alpha before his disappearance.
- **2012**: Government Career - Recruited by the intelligence services as a junior behavioral scientist.
- **2016**: Senior Tracker - Promoted to international intelligence tracking, analyzing rogue agents globally.
- **2020**: London Yacht Trap - Survived a staged yacht sinking orchestrated by Malhotra; rescued by a masked protector.
- **2020**: Detonation Escape - Confronted Alpha in a RAW facility, escaped during Malhotra's building demolition, and vanished into a shadow flight.
