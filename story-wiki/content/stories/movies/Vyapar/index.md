# Vyapar

**Status:** Idea
**Format:** Film, Web Series
**Genre:** Crime / Thriller / Dark Comedy
**Working Premise:** A Squid Game-style story where a group of family and friends unknowingly become players in a deadly Monopoly-like power game — until betrayal and murder reveal who the real 'Maalik' is.
**Date Written:** October 21, 2025
**Inspiration:** Squid Game + Monopoly board structure

## Synopsis

A series like Squid Game — but built on the game of Monopoly / Business, played out within a real family and friend group.

All players are unknowingly caught in a web of greed, pride, and power play that escalates into a murder mystery. No one realises they are participants in a deadly Monopoly game until it is too late.

**Sub-arcs:**
- Girlfriend arc
- Friends' betrayal
- The reveal of *Maalik* (the unseen orchestrator)

**Tone:** Suspenseful. The game's rules slowly override the players' real-world moral code.
