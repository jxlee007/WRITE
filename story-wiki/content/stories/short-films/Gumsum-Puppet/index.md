# Gumsum Puppet

**Status:** Idea (Polished Synopsis)
**Format:** Short Film, Film
**Genre:** Drama / Tragedy
**Working Premise:** A world-renowned puppet maker falls obsessively in love with his greatest creation — until a disastrous performance shatters both the puppet and his illusion.
**Date Written:** December 29, 2025
**Inspiration:** Song "Gumsum Gumsum"

---

## Synopsis

A master puppet maker, celebrated across the world for his craft, crosses from artistry into obsession. His most recent creation — an extraordinarily lifelike puppet — becomes the object of his total fixation. He believes, at some level, that the puppet is real. Or perhaps more accurately: he needs it to be.

The breaking point arrives during a public performance. Something goes catastrophically wrong. The puppet is destroyed. And with it, the maker's carefully constructed illusion — of love returned, of artistry made flesh — is shattered.

## Characters

- **The Puppet Maker** — protagonist; world-renowned, deeply isolated, obsessive
- **The Puppet** — the object of obsession; extraordinarily crafted; effectively a character

## Structure

Short film / film format — single-arc, classical tragedy structure:
1. The maker at the height of his craft and obsession
2. The performance — public, high-stakes
3. The shattering — puppet destroyed; illusion exposed
4. Aftermath — what remains

## Status & Notes

Very polished synopsis with a clear arc and settled theme. One of the most emotionally complete ideas in the catalog. Well-suited to a short film format.

The "Gumsum Gumsum" song inspiration suggests a melancholy, haunting tone — silent protagonist, expressive craft work.

**Open Questions:**
- What exactly goes wrong at the performance? Mechanical failure? A revelation?
- Is there an audience character who shatters the illusion by reacting to the puppet as an object?
- Does the maker survive the story, or is this a literal tragedy?
