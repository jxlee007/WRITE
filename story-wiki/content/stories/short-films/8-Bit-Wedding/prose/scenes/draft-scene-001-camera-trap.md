# Scene 001 — Camera Trap

*Opening scene. The game has already started. Nobody knows it has started.*

**Location:** A haveli in Amritsar. A large Indian wedding, Day 1. The courtyard is full — string lights, marigolds, a DJ in the corner who has just been told to turn it down by four different aunties. The Groom is standing near the entrance.
**Characters:** Navneet (the groom, 28, would rather be anywhere, is aware this makes him a bad person, is managing), the Player (V.O. only — the game announcer, heard only by the audience)
**Purpose:** The 8-bit wedding begins. The gamer overlay drops into a real Indian wedding and the comedy of recognition — every social move, every family interaction, every ritual — mapped onto game mechanics. The scene functions as both satire and genuine warmth.

---

*[8-BIT STARTUP SOUND]*

*[TITLE CARD: SHAADI SIMULATOR 3000. PRESS START.]*

*[START PRESSED.]*

---

The bus from Delhi was late, which meant thirty-seven relatives arrived simultaneously at 4 PM, which meant the entrance to the haveli transformed instantly from a space into an event.

Navneet was standing at the gate because his mother had positioned him there forty minutes ago and told him not to move, and he hadn't moved, and now he was shaking hands and touching feet and receiving blessings in an unbroken sequence that had the quality of a very elaborate conveyor belt.

*[ANNOUNCER (V.O., 8-bit voice chip, enthusiastic):* **NEW GUEST DETECTED. INITIATING SHAADI PROTOCOL.**

*Guest identified: Tau ji. Relationship: Father's elder brother. Status: LEGENDARY ELDER. Recommended action: Touch feet immediately. Delay penalty: -20 IZZAT POINTS.]*

Navneet touched Tau ji's feet.

*[IZZAT POINTS: +15. ACHIEVEMENT UNLOCKED: "Respectful Newphew." Tau ji blessing received: "Khush raho." BUFF ACTIVE: +5 PATIENCE for 30 minutes.]*

Tau ji moved on. The next guest appeared.

*[NEW GUEST DETECTED. CHACHI BEENA. Relationship: Mother's brother's wife's sister. Warning: HIGH CHEEK-PINCH PROBABILITY. Evasive action recommended. Window: 0.8 seconds.]*

Navneet was too slow.

*[CHEEK PINCH LANDED. DAMAGE: -3 DIGNITY. Cannot be resisted. Chachi Beena is LEGENDARY TIER. All attacks unavoidable.]*

"Hai kitna handsome ho gaya," Chachi Beena said, holding his face.

"Ji, Chachi ji."

*[CORRECT RESPONSE SELECTED. +5 IZZAT. CONVERSATION CONCLUDED.]*

She moved into the haveli.

The line continued. Behind Navneet, his cousin Bunty materialized from somewhere and stood beside him with the companionable silence of someone who had also been placed at a gate and told not to move.

"This is a lot," Bunty said.

"It's a wedding," Navneet said.

"It's an *attack,*" Bunty said.

*[BUNTY JOINS YOUR PARTY. +1 ALLY. Combined IZZAT pool increased.]*

"Navneet," his mother said, arriving from nowhere. "Your maami is here. She's brought her daughter."

*[CRITICAL EVENT TRIGGERED. THE DAUGHTER-COMPARISON SEQUENCE BEGINS. WARNING: COMPARISON WILL BE UNFAVOURABLE REGARDLESS OF ACTUAL METRICS. SURVIVAL STRATEGY: SMILE, NOD, REDIRECT.]*

"Which maami?" Navneet said.

"*The* maami."

*[LOADING... MAAMI SUDHA. STATUS: FINAL BOSS (FAMILY TIER). Known abilities: Unsolicited advice, salary enquiry, strategic deployment of "your cousin Rahul," disapproval aura. PASSIVE: Your achievements are 40% less impressive in her presence.]*

Maami Sudha arrived in a purple salwar kameez and immediately looked at Navneet's face with the specific expression of someone doing a market valuation.

"He's looking thin," she said to his mother, about Navneet, in front of Navneet.

"He's been busy," his mother said.

"Rahul ate properly even during his CA exams," Maami Sudha said. To no one. To everyone. To the universe.

*[RAHUL INVOKED. COMPARISON DAMAGE: -10 CONFIDENCE. Rahul: Not present. Rahul's achievements: +30% in his absence. Recommended response: Change subject.]*

"Maami ji," Navneet said, "Bunty was just asking about your shawl — where did you get it?"

*[REDIRECT SUCCESSFUL. MAAMI SUDHA DISTRACTED. +20 TACTICAL POINTS. BUNTY TAKES DAMAGE: -15 SHAWL-DISCUSSION PATIENCE. BUNTY: "you owe me."]*

Bunty looked at Navneet with the expression of someone who had just been used as a human shield.

"I hate you," Bunty said.

"Family," Navneet said.

*[WEDDING SURVIVAL: Day 1, Hour 0:47. Current stats: IZZAT: 76/100. PATIENCE: 43/100. DIGNITY: 71/100. OBJECTIVES REMAINING: 34. BOSS ENCOUNTERS REMAINING: 7. Days to Main Event: 2.]*

*[YOU ARE NOT READY. BUT NO ONE EVER IS.]*

*[GAME CONTINUES.]*

---

**Consequence:** Navneet survives the arrival sequence. He has, in the process, accidentally committed to sitting next to Maami Sudha at dinner, performed the feet-touching ritual for twenty-six people, received eleven blessings of varying sincerity, and eaten three laddoos he didn't want from three different aunties who would have been offended if he'd refused. The game has logged all of this. The Mother-in-Law introduction is scheduled for tomorrow morning. It is flagged as a **SPECIAL STAGE.**
