# Orangutan Tiger

**Status:** Idea (Full Three-Act Structure)
**Format:** Anime, Film
**Genre:** Adventure / Family / Allegory
**Working Premise:** A tiger cub raised by an orangutan must reunite his estranged brothers and defend their jungle from a human invasion — guided by the values of the mother who raised him.
**Date Written:** No date listed
**Inspiration:** Lion King + Jungle Book

## Synopsis

A tiger cub is raised by an orangutan mother after being separated from his kin. He grows up between two worlds — tiger nature and orangutan values. When his estranged brothers must be reunited and the jungle threatened by human invasion, he is the only one capable of bridging both sides.
