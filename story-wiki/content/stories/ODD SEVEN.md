# ODD SEVEN

**Status:** Idea
**Format:** Film, Web Series
**Genre:** Action / Sci-Fi / Thriller
**Working Premise:** Seven people across the globe share the same face but different fates — and only one of them can prevent the others from destroying each other.
**Date Written:** July 17, 2022
**Inspiration:** Juice WRLD

## Synopsis

Seven individuals worldwide are born with identical faces but radically different lives. The story explores what it means to share an identity you didn't choose — and what happens when seven versions of "you" are on a collision course. Only one can step up to prevent total destruction.
