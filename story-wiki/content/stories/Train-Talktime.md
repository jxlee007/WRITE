# Train Talktime

**Status:** Idea
**Format:** Web Series
**Genre:** Slice of Life / Comedy / Drama
**Working Premise:** An anthology series of 10–30 minute episodes where strangers share stories, secrets, and laughter aboard Indian trains.
**Date Written:** December 3, 2025

## Synopsis

A repeatable IP: an anthology series of 10–30 minute episodes, each sharing a unique human experience set inside Indian trains.

**Episode 1:** Two aunties from different families discuss a corrupt death — and find unexpected unity across religious lines, in contrast to how unity looks on paper.

**Planned Long-Format Specials (1 hr each):**
- School Party
- First College Trip
- Yaar Bathere (Many Friends)

**Tone:** Warm, grounded, conversational. The train is the equaliser — social class, religion, age boundaries dissolve in transit.

## Themes

- **Human Connection in Transit** — trains as the great equalizer of Indian society
- **Strangers and Stories** — the anthology format as theme
