# Villain Hierarchy — Revelation Order

The story's antagonist structure is a four-layer onion. Each layer is the true threat while the previous layer is resolved — and each layer recontextualizes everything before it.

---

## Layer 1 — Daimler (♠ King)

**Season:** 1
**Presented as:** Cold elimination machine burning through competition with 3D chess domain.
**Apparent end:** Eliminated/defeated at Season 1's conclusion.
**Truth (revealed later):** He Awakened, was excommunicated as a Waker, and cast to the human world. He was never truly defeated — he exceeded the system's capacity.
**Ultimate role:** Tragic architect of the escape mechanism. Inventor of the Inheritance Technique. Villain who becomes the door others walk through.

---

## Layer 2 — The Diamond King (♦ King)

**Season:** 3–4
**Presented as:** Leader of the Betrayers faction. Mastermind behind the tournament conspiracy.
**Goal:** Hijack or control the tournament outcome — possibly to redirect the harvest for his own benefit rather than the Revis.
**Profile:** TBD — full character file not yet created.

---

## Layer 3 — Chains of Fate

**Season:** Post-competition arc (after Phantom 12 story is established)
**Presented as:** Historical outside card, already sealed by Grand-Emo Storm. Re-emerges.
**Classification:** Fate-based power user. Predates the current competition cycle. Its sealing cost Emo his competitive career.
**Scale:** Operates at a threat level above the tournament's normal power ceiling.
**Profile:** Needs Wakers integration and expanded arc.

---

## Layer 4 — The Shoshinsha Creator (The Satyug Architect)

**Season:** Final revelation — only after Phantom 12's story is fully established
**Presented as:** The origin of everything.
**What he is:** A being from Satyug who extracted souls through psychic ritual to create the Shoshinsha Realm (3D globe of revolving threads). Transcended to astral plane. His physical body, left in a cave frozen in a Time Psychosis pocket, is the anchor keeping the realm alive.
**The rule:** Destroy the body → the realm dissolves → all souls freed.
**Why he matters:** The Revis are not the root problem. The entire tournament system — cards, Sonapers, competition, harvesting — is downstream of his original soul extraction. He is the first cause.

---

## The Chain of Knowledge — How the Truth Travels

```
The Creator freezes his body in a cave (Satyug)
        ↓
Daimler's Lvl 3 time-travels to the frozen moment
Daimler witnesses the body, understands the origin
Daimler cannot destroy the body himself (Unanchored, locked out)
        ↓
Daimler gives Phantom 12 the hint:
"Only someone whose power works in the human realm can do this.
 That person is you."
        ↓
Phantom 12 — while in a situation crossing between human world
and fantasy realm — forces his power as a CLAW through the 2D
boundary of the realm, reaching into the physical world
        ↓
The Claw destroys the Creator's physical body
        ↓
The Shoshinsha Realm resolves
All souls freed — Resonance Chain with their descendants
The tournament's reason for existing dissolves
```

---

## The Resolution — What "Ending" Actually Means

The series does not end by winning the tournament. It ends by **destroying the tournament's foundation.**

True winners (Resonance Unchain survivors → 3D return) escape the system *while it still runs.*
The Phantom 12 conclusion ends the system itself — for every future cycle, for every soul still inside.

These are two different victories:
- Sato's circle: personal escape (Resonance Unchain + Daimler's map)
- Phantom 12: systemic dissolution (Shoshinsha Creator destroyed)

The full ending requires both.
