## Core Traits

- Paint-based fighter — uses a pot and brush as weapons
- Diamonds house affiliation suggests deception and manipulation as underlying themes
- Art-as-weapon aesthetic: paint as suspended hazard, then as active projectile, then as self-transformation

---

## Power Levels

- **Lvl 1:** Suspends paint — freezes paint droplets in mid-air as a field of floating hazards
- **Lvl 2:** Dangerous paint globs — spawns large, volatile paint masses; likely explosive or corrosive
- **Lvl 3:** Transforms into paint — the fighter dissolves into the medium itself; unpredictable, fluid, nearly impossible to track
