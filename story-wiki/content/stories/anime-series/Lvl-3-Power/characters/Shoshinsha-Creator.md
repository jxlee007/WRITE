# The Creator — The Satyug Architect

**Appears In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../index.md)
**Role:** Ultimate Villain (Series Final Revelation)
**Card Rank:** None — predates the card system entirely
**Classification:** Origin Being / Astral Entity
**Era:** Satyug (the first age — millions of years before the story's present)
**Status:** Body alive, frozen in time. Consciousness transcended to astral plane.

> **Reveal timing:** Introduced after the Phantom 12 story is fully established. The final layer of the villain hierarchy, revealed long after Daimler, Diamond King, and Chains of Fate.

---

## Origin — What He Did

In the age of Satyug, a powerful being — motivated by nothing more than his own imagination and desire for creation — performed a ritual. He extracted the souls of many people through mental/psychic manipulation and used them as raw material to construct the **Shoshinsha Realm**: a 3D globe of revolving threads, each thread a compressed soul.

He then transcended into the astral plane — leaving the physical world behind — but maintained a living connection to the realm through his physical body, left behind in a cave. The body is the anchor.

> **The rule:** The Shoshinsha Realm cannot be resolved or destroyed until his physical body is physically destroyed. The realm exists because the body exists.

---

## The Frozen Cave

After transcending, his body entered a state of deep meditation. The meditation triggered what would later be called **Time Psychosis** — the specific space around his body froze. That moment in time stopped moving entirely within the cave's boundary.

- The body has not aged. The cave has not changed. The moment is locked.
- This frozen point in time is **visitable** — Daimler's Lvl 3 Time Infliction can reach it.
- Daimler visits the cave, witnesses the body, and through the temporal context, understands the truth of the Shoshinsha Realm's origin.

---

## Why He Cannot Be Stopped By Normal Means

- He is not in the fantasy realm. He is in the astral plane.
- His body is in a frozen time pocket — unreachable by normal space-time movement.
- The body cannot be fought in a conventional sense — there is no battle. It must simply be physically destroyed.
- His power level in the astral plane is incomprehensible relative to any tournament-era fighter.
- **Only someone who can project force across the realm boundary — from inside the 2D tournament system outward into the physical world — can reach the body.**

That person is Phantom 12.

---

## The Connection to the Harvesting Truth

The Shoshinsha Realm was built on stolen souls. The entire tournament system — the cards, the Sonapers, the competition, the Revis harvesting — is downstream of this original crime. The Revis are not the root problem. They are parasites on a system that was itself built on extraction.

Resolving the Shoshinsha Realm does not just free the souls inside it. It dissolves the entire framework that made the tournament possible. The ending is not just winning the competition. It is ending the competition's reason for existing.
