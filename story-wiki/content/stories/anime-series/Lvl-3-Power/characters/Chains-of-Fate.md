# Chains of Fate

**Appears In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../../stories/Lvl-3-Power.md)
**Role:** Antagonist (historical) / Outside Card
**Card Status:** Outside Card Structure — no longer in active competition

---

## Core Traits

- Fate-based power user — a character who controls destiny itself
- Defeated [Grand-Emo Storm](Grand-Emo-Storm.md) in a legendary battle, forcing his retirement from competition
- Exists as an Outside Card: participated in a past competition but is no longer an active contestant

---

## Power Levels

- **Lvl 1:** Fate Steal — alters or seizes the fated outcome of an opponent
- **Lvl 2:** Death Palm — eyes open in palms; can see and instantly deliver death on touch
- **Lvl 3:** Fate Demon

---

## The Sealing Battle

When young, [Grand-Emo Storm](Grand-Emo-Storm.md) (then known as Grenadier Emo) fought Chains of Fate to seal its power. Emo used **Mastery of Seals** combined with the "Shirdi power" — feeding the demon energy into an "eyescape" that changed form based on extracted power, converting it into chain links.

The cost to Emo was permanent: using Mastery of Seals depleted him enough to effectively retire from the competition. Chains of Fate paid with the "death palms" — its most lethal ability.
