## Core Traits

- Emo's disciple — trained alongside [Sato](Sato.md) and [Skatze](Skatze.md)
- Stealth and illusion specialist within the raw-power Clubs house — an unusual outlier
- The mask motif in the name suggests concealment, misdirection, and identity switching as core mechanics
- **Identity & Gender Reveal:** Due to her concealment and mask skills, she appears completely gender-neutral to the other contestants throughout the series. Her female identity is kept hidden, eventually revealed to Sato by Grand-Emo Storm in a conversation.

---

## Role in Lvl 3 Power

Maskarray is one of [Grand-Emo Storm](Grand-Emo-Storm.md)'s key disciples, trained alongside [Sato](Sato.md) (♣K) and [Skatze](Skatze.md) (♠A). As ♣6, she operates in the mid-tier of the Clubs house — close enough to the top to matter, far enough to have room to grow.

The stealth/illusion kit inside a brute-power house makes Maskarray a tactical anomaly: when everyone else is swinging hard, Maskarray is already behind you.
