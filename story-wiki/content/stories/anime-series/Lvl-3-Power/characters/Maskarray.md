## Role in Lvl 3 Power

Maskarray is one of [Grand-Emo Storm](Grand-Emo-Storm.md)'s key disciples, trained alongside [Sato](Sato.md) (♣K) and [Skatze](Skatze.md) (♠A). As ♣6, he operates in the mid-tier of the Clubs house — close enough to the top to matter, far enough to have room to grow.

The stealth/illusion kit inside a brute-power house makes Maskarray a tactical anomaly: when everyone else is swinging hard, Maskarray is already behind you.
