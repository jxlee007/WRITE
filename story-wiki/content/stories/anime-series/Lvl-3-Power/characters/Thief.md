# Thief

**Appears In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../../stories/Lvl-3-Power.md)
**Role:** Fighter / Supporting
**Card Rank:** ♦ 8 (Diamonds 8) — confirmed
**House:** Diamonds (Betrayers / Intelligence / Manipulation)

---

## Core Traits

- Theft-based power user — the only character in the universe who can steal time and skills
- Diamonds house archetype: a literal thief, operating through deception and appropriation
- Style: infiltration, ambush, and stripping opponents of their advantages

---

## Power Levels

- **Lvl 1:** Translucent — becomes partially or fully invisible; stealth entry into any situation
- **Lvl 2:** Time Theft — steals time from opponents or the environment; exact mechanics TBD
- **Lvl 3:** Skill Theft — permanently or temporarily copies/removes an opponent's ability; the most dangerous power set in the Diamonds house at numbered rank

---

## Notes

- Skill Theft at Lvl 3 makes Thief theoretically the most versatile fighter in the universe — the power ceiling depends on what skills have been encountered
