# Phantom 12

**Appears In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../../stories/Lvl-3-Power.md)
**Role:** Wildcard / Joker 2 / Series Final Resolution Agent
**Card Rank:** Joker 2 — Outside normal hierarchy
**Spin-off Candidate:** Yes ⭐
**Classification:** Cross-Realm Power User — uniquely able to project force from the fantasy realm into the physical human world

> **Narrative function:** The only person in the story whose power works in the human realm. Becomes the instrument by which the Shoshinsha Realm is destroyed and all extracted souls are freed. Daimler cannot do it — he tells Phantom 12 who can.

---

## Core Traits

- Ghost-summoning fighter who exploits the century-long competition cycle in ways no one else has
- Operates outside the normal rules of the Shoshinsha competition
- Joker status: unpredictable, dangerous precisely because they seem non-threatening
- Flagged as the strongest spin-off candidate in the universe

---

## Power Levels

- **Lvl 1:** Ghost Summon — summons a single ghost entity
- **Lvl 2:** All 12 Ghosts — calls all 12 ghosts simultaneously; the "12" in the name is literal
- **Lvl 3:** Inner Fate Form — physical transformation: blue eyes, everything else pure white; represents the moment all ghost-selves merge into one vessel

---

## Spin-off Potential

Phantom 12 is noted as having "scope to develop into a separate live action series." The century-competition cycle exploitation angle provides a standalone narrative: someone who has figured out how to game the system across multiple cycles, accumulating power across centuries.

---

## The Final Act — Destroying the Shoshinsha Creator

The Shoshinsha Realm was built by an ancient being from Satyug who extracted souls through psychic ritual. That being transcended to the astral plane, leaving his physical body in a cave frozen in a Time Psychosis pocket. The body is the anchor — while it exists, the realm exists and all souls remain trapped.

**Daimler's role:** Through his Lvl 3 time-travel, Daimler visits the frozen cave. He sees the body. He understands the truth. He cannot act — he is Unanchored, locked out of the tournament system, unable to project force across the realm boundary. He finds Phantom 12 and delivers one piece of information:

> *"Only someone whose power works in the human realm can reach that body. That is you."*

**Phantom 12's resolution:** In a situation where he is crossing between the human world and the fantasy realm, Phantom 12 forces his power through the 2D boundary of the realm as a **Claw** — a concentrated projection from inside the tournament system outward into the physical world. The Claw reaches the Creator's frozen cave and destroys the physical body.

The result:
- The Shoshinsha Realm dissolves
- All extracted souls are freed
- The freed souls resonate with their descendants (Resonance Chain)
- The tournament's foundational reason for existing is permanently ended

**Why only Phantom 12:** His powers function in the human realm — the only fighter in the story for whom this is true. In the fantasy realm, his power level is off-charts. The cross-realm capability is what makes this possible. Specifics of how this works TBD — next session.
