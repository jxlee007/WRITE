## Arc Notes

### Season 3: The Prequel Conflict
* **Arc 1 — Leaves Arc (Clubs vs Spades):** Kaien Mugen (♣ Ace) and Skatze (♠ Ace) established. Combat mechanics introduced.
* **Season 3 Finale:** Sato undergoes the Shoshinsha ritual, passes the Storm of Changes (first mortal), and enters the tournament as King of Clubs.

### Season 4: Sato's Rise & The Betrayal War
* **Arc 2 — Conspiracy Arc:** Sato climbs ranks. Betrayers plot. Daimler targets Kaien.
* **Arc 3 — Spades Arc (The Tragedy):** Daimler assassinates Kaien. Clown is framed. Skatze vows silent vengeance.
* **Arc 4 — Hearts Arc (Power Brawl):** Skatze contacts Sato. King of Hearts killed in Null Realm. Sato witnesses his final breath.
* **Arc 5 — Diamonds Arc:** Final showdown. Betrayers exposed.

### Part 2: Post-Competition
