## Arc Notes

### Part 1: The Competition
* **Start to Mid Competition (Arcs 1 & 2):** Sato's ascension through the Storm of Changes Arc (Arc 1). Sato enters the Leaves Arc (Arc 2), meeting Skatze. Dijay Hans (disguised as a normal player) withdraws silently off-screen from the active brackets.
* **Mid Competition (Arcs 3 & 4):** The Spades Arc (Arc 3) where the Betrayers emerge, and the Hearts Arc (Arc 4) where the King of Hearts is murdered in the Null Realm and Sato finds him on his final breath.
* **End Competition (Arc 5):** The final Diamonds Arc showdown against the Diamond King.

### Part 2: The Post-Competition Situation
* **Post-Competition Arc:** Dijay Hans recruits Sato and initiates a brutal, Levi-like training regime to prepare him for a system-wide threat beyond the tournament.
