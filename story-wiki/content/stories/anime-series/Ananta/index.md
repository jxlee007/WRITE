# Ananta

**Status:** Pilot Script Complete
**Format:** Web Series, Anime
**Genre:** Cyberpunk / Sci-Fi / Action Thriller

---

## Synopsis

In a near-future India governed by an omnipresent AI mesh, a youth awakening with experimental neural firmware and a broken ex-spy contractor cross paths in the shadows of a vertical city, each learning to bend the system's rules before deciding whether to fix it, fracture it, or burn it down.

---

## Tone & Aesthetic

A cyberpunk anime aesthetic featuring gritty street-level intimacy:
* Warm but tense human stakes set against cold systemic control.
* Kinetic spectacle balanced by quiet, emotional introspection.
* Themes of agency, consent, and memory.

---

## Factions & Setting
* **Upstack / Downstack**: The vertical stratification of the city.
* **CIVILNET**: The omnipresent AI mesh governing the city, enforcing rules through the Decree system.

---
