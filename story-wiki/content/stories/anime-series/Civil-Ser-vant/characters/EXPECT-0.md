# EXPECT 0

**Story:** [Civil Ser-vant](../index.md)
**Type:** Character
**Genre:** Cyberpunk

---

## Identity

EXPECT 0 is a legendary, unpredictable rogue operative who exists entirely outside the factions of Upstack and Downstack. He has zero loyalties, zero fear, and zero interest in the ideological battles of the Mesh. He works strictly on his own terms—for the task, for the thrill, and for his own fun.

---

## Core Traits

- **Absolute Solitude:** Trusted by no one and trusting of no one. He operates in complete isolation, refusing to allow anyone to get close enough to hack, influence, or manipulate him.
- **Apex Precision:** Functions at the absolute highest calibre of operatives. His mechanical execution and hacking capability are flawless, contrasting sharply with his erratic personal behavior.
- **Chaos-Driven (Hedonist):** Does not care about survival, legacy, or order. He lives purely for fun and the thrill of the moment until his natural end, making him a statistical anomaly the Mesh cannot predict.
- **Will-Driven:** Operates entirely by his own fluctuating moral compass, completely ignoring the decrees of the Mesh and the politics of human factions.

---

## Psychology & Backstory

### The Foundational Wound: The Upstack Expansion Massacre
As a child, EXPECT 0 witnessed the brutal reality of the Upstack colonization. The authorities marketed the expansion as a "necessary sacrifice for the greater good of Earth," but when the street-level residents resisted, they responded with a horrific, AI-driven massacre. 
He watched automated combat systems ruthlessly slaughter civilians under human orders, noting the absolute, chilling lack of hesitation in the machines. This shattered his trust in systems, progress, and humanity. He realized that in the eyes of the machine, human life is valued at zero—and thus, he chose to value the system and its rules at zero as well.

### The Defensive Shift: Deviating Presence
To protect himself, he developed elite combat and hacking skills, but emotionally, he detached completely. Rather than hiding in fear, he chose to become an unpredictable specter. He turned his complete lack of self-preservation into a weapon, deliberately deviating his presence to keep others constantly worried about what he will do next, while he remains entirely unbothered.

### The Backstory Link: The Ghost Spirits
His origins are tied to the **Ghost Spirits** collective ([Cyberpunk Haunting Spirits](../ideas/Cyberpunk-Haunting-Spirits.md)), a group that hacks bio-interfaces and puppets humans like dolls. He grew up in the shadow of their anti-government resistance, but grew disillusioned with their compromised ideals. Today, he is their most successful "failed" asset—a phantom who occasionally works with them but can never be controlled or predicted by them. The protagonist first learns of EXPECT 0's existence by uncovering his hidden roots within the Ghost Spirits' networks.

## Core Philosophy

> *Can't expect anything from him. Can expect everything to happen because of him.*

## Narrative Destiny

If EXPECT 0's path leads to death, his end will not be caused by hardware degradation, software virus, or firmware burnout. He refuses to let the technology of the Mesh or the upgrades in his body dictate his end. If he dies, it will be a purely organic reality or a deliberate, laughing choice of his own making—going out entirely on his own terms.
