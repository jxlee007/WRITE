# Mission: Impossible Date

An action-comedy pitch about a guy who is set up on a Bumble date and must face action-thriller obstacles to reach and survive his date.

## Pitch Concept
A guy who is set up for the date needs to reach his date, but certain circumstances make it a kind of action thriller. Eventually, the guy reaches the date, but action-thriller events happen there too, becoming the ultimate experience of the date and proving the line:
> "You have to be a fighter to be a lover. If you can't, then what kind of lover are you if you can't fight for your love?"

Between start and end, there are two events between afternoon and evening.
First, a collision with another vehicle where the hero sorts the matter with a fight right there and moves on.
He reaches the destination without being aware that the goons would follow him. Before they arrive, there is some exchange between the hero and the girl.
Then, a second fight breaks out inside the cafe between the goons and the hero.

## Updated Beats

1. **Bumble Notification**: Hero gets a Bumble notification — date at 7:00 PM in Bandra.
2. **3:00 PM**: Jumps on his bike. Rain starts pouring; clock is ticking while riding through heavy traffic.
3. **Collision**: Bike hits a goon's car at a traffic signal.
4. **First Fight**: Goon gets out aggressive — Hero fights him right there, wins, and moves on.
5. **Cab Ride**: Catches a cab, but gets stuck in traffic.
6. **First Text Exchange**:
   - Girl: *"You close? 😊"*
   - Hero: *"Almost there"* (bleeding knuckle, soaked, but grinning)
7. **Arrival**: Arrives at the café at 6:58 PM — looking destroyed but standing.
8. **First Sight**: Girl sees him, but pretends she is unconfirmed whether it is actually him.
9. **Introduction**:
   - Girl: *"You look terrible."*
   - Hero: *"But I'm here."*
10. **Sweet Moment**: Sweet moment begins, and chai is ordered.
11. **Goon's Return**: The goon walks in, having followed the hero to the café.
12. **Second Fight**: Fight breaks out inside the café. The hero protects the girl.
13. **Climax**: Goon is knocked down. Other customers are left stunned.
14. **Resolution**: Hero sits back, fixes his collar: *"Sorry about that."*
15. **Reaction**: The girl stares, then laughs — a real, unguarded laugh.
16. **Ending Dialogue**:
    - Girl: *"Most guys would've cancelled."*
    - Hero: *"Most guys didn't want to meet you this badly."*
