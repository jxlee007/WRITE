# God and Demon Within

**Status:** Concept Fragment (Seed)
**Format:** Dark Fantasy
**Genre:** Dark Fantasy / Supernatural / Internal Conflict

---

## Premise

A character — or story — built around the fundamental dual-nature of a protagonist who houses both divine and demonic forces within themselves. The central tension is not an external villain but an **internal war of natures**.

---

## Core Concept

The "god and demon within" framing suggests a protagonist who:
- Cannot be classified as good or evil
- Has power that manifests differently depending on which nature dominates at a given moment
- Must constantly manage the coexistence of two opposing cosmic forces in a single body

This connects to the catalog's broader interest in **identity as a contested space** — the protagonist is not choosing between values but between natures.
