# Realm of Shads

**Used In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../stories/Lvl-3-Power.md)

---

## Overview

A dimension separate from the human world (Earth). It is home to beings called **Sonapers** — entities that share the characteristics of human personality but have no tangible physical form. The Realm of Shads is the origin point for the [52 Card Universe](52-Card-Universe.md) competition.

---

## Sonapers

Non-physical beings whose personalities mirror human archetypes:
- Combat hunger, domain mastery, defence, manipulation, etc.
- Cannot interact with the material world on their own
- Powers remain dormant unless the Sonaper holds the title of **Revis**

---

## Revis

The highest position among Sonapers. Key properties:
- The Sonaper who attains Revis has their powers fully unlocked
- Only one (or a select group) can hold this title at a time
- The Revis can be challenged and replaced

---

## The Competition (held every century)

The Shoshinsha Competition runs on a century cycle:

1. Sonapers who wish to challenge for Revis are gathered in the Realm of Shads
2. They undergo the ritual of **SHOSHINSHA** — a transformation that converts each Sonaper into a physical card
3. Upon transformation, each card attains a colour representing one of 5 power levels — the Sonaper's proximity to becoming Revis:
   > Lvl 1 → Lvl 2 → Lvl 3 → Lvl 4 → Lvl 5 (Revis)
4. The cards (Shoshinsha) are taken out of the Realm of Shads into Earth
5. Cards spread across the world — each card is drawn automatically toward a human whose aura matches the Sonaper's personality; the card bonds to that human, granting them the Sonaper's power set
6. The competition then unfolds among the bonded humans on Earth

## Outside Card Structure

Some Sonapers participated in past competitions but are no longer active contestants. They exist in the realm as retired or defeated legends. Their cards are no longer in circulation on Earth.

Examples:
- [Grand-Emo Storm](../characters/cards/Grand-Emo-Storm.md) — retired after *Chains of Fate* defeat
- [Master Shin](../characters/cards/Master-Shin.md) — retired trainer
- [Chains of Fate](../characters/cards/Chains-of-Fate.md) — the one who forced Grand-Emo's retirement
