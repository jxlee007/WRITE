# Power Level System Design

**Used In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../stories/Lvl 3 Power.md), [Burning Punches and Frozen Kicks](../stories/Burning Punches and Frozen Kicks.md)

---

## The Pattern

Both major battle-fantasy stories use a **tiered power level system** where each character has defined stages of ability that unlock through story progression. This is a direct inheritance from shonen anime conventions (JJK's domain expansion, Black Clover's magic, Avatar's bending mastery).

---

## Lvl 3 Power — Meric Expansion System

- **3 levels per fighter:** Lvl 1 (raw/base), Lvl 2 (advanced technique), Lvl 3 (domain extension)
- **Domain = "Meric Expansion"** — replaces the reality fabric within a range; the fighter's personal universe
- Two shape variants for domains:
  - **Cube Linear** — square blocked terrain; predictable but powerful
  - **Triangle Polygon** — criss-cross pattern; harder to understand, harder to break
- **Breaking from inside:** requires energy equal to every domain layer summon simultaneously — the true test of an enemy's power
- **Energy cost scales with layers** — the more reality layers, the more fragile and energy-intensive
- **The Ace of Spades question:** He asks enemies "square or triangle?" — then constructs the opposite to maximally confuse them. If they can't answer, he assumes they don't understand his limits.

---

## Burning Punches & Frozen Kicks — Elemental Mastery System

- Powers: Fire (Flaming Fists) and Ice (Frozen Kicks)
- Progression mirrors life stages: childhood (raw manifestation), teenage (technique development), adulthood (full mastery / war-level)
- Power growth is implicit through the three-arc structure

---

## Design Principles (Craft Notes)

1. **Clarity of levels** — the audience should always know where a fighter sits in the hierarchy
2. **Costs and weaknesses** — the best power systems in the catalog have explicit costs (energy drain, retirement, one-use-only)
3. **Domain as identity** — in Lvl 3 Power, domains express the user's inner world (Grand-Emo's purple tornado = never-give-up; Ace of Spades' spatial split = a mind that divides and confuses)
4. **The underdog's path** — [Sato](../characters/Sato.md) rose through this system without natural advantage; the system should be winnable from below

---

## Cross-Story Potential

The tiered power system is a signature tendency in this catalog. If a shared battle universe is ever assembled across stories, this system provides a ready template.
