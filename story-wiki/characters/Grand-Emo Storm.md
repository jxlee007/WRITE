# Grand-Emo Storm

**Appears In:** [Lvl 3 Power](../stories/Lvl 3 Power.md)
**Role:** Supporting — Retired Champion / Reluctant Trainer
**Card Rank:** Former competitor; now trainer outside the active competition
**Inspiration:** Isaac Netero (Hunter x Hunter)
**Young name:** Grenadier Emo

---

## Core Traits

- Carries an emotion-based power system — the most psychologically unusual kit in the story
- Deeply reluctant to train anyone — though he produces exceptional disciples
- Hates being a trainer, but does it anyway (classic reluctant master archetype)
- Retired not by choice but by cost: his final battle permanently depleted his ability to use Mastery of Seals

---

## Power Levels

- **Lvl 1 — Stick of Emotion:** Touch someone and fix one emotion onto them at power-boost intensity
- **Lvl 2 — Wind of Emotion:** Those who smell the wind are affected by one emotion directed at another
- **Lvl 3 — Domain: Tover Realm** — sand dune mountains with a hut where Emo lives; far off in the dunes, a broad purple sandstorm/tornado that never fades (symbolizes never-give-up)

---

## The Sealing Incident (Retirement Story)

When young (Grenadier Emo), he fought a "Fare Demon" to seal its power. He used **Mastery of Seals** combined with the "Shirdi power" — feeding demon energy into an "eyescape" that changed form based on the extracted power, converting it into chain links.

**The cost:** Using Mastery of Seals once takes such a toll on the user that Emo effectively retired from the competition afterwards. The demon paid with the "death palms" (kills living things with just their weight/touch). Emo was depleted enough to be "kind of defeated" — forced into the trainer role he resents.

## Arc

The retired legend who shaped the protagonist from behind the scenes. His "never-give-up" tornado in his domain is the emotional thesis he passes on — even if he never says it directly.
